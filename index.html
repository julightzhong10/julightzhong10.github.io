About Me

I am an Algorithm Engineer at the AI Lab of SAIC Motor. Before moving to industry career, I was an Master student at UCLA Computer Science Department.

I foucs on the vision perception solutions for production-level autonomous driving including L2/3 ADAS, L4 Robotaxi, and DMS. I am also engaged in machine learning research, recently in Multi-views(BEV) 3D vision perception, monocular 2/3D vision perception, self-supervised/representation learning.
