About Me

I am an Algorithm Engineer at the AI Lab of SAIC Motor. Before moving to industry career, I was a Master student at UCLA Computer Science Department.

I work on the vision perception solutions for production-level autonomous driving. I also do research in ML/CV problems for fun, recently in Multi-views(BEV) 3D vision perception, camera modeling, and self-supervised/representation learning.
