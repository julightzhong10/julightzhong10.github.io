About me

I am an Algorithm Engineer at the Artificial Intelligence Lab of SAIC Motor. Before moving to industry career, I receivced my Master degree in Computer Science from UCLA and Bachelor degree in Computing from Macao Polytechnic Institute.

I am interested in many areas of machine learning, perception, and intelligence methods. My recent works lie in unsupervised representation learning, object detection, generative modeling, attention mechanism and their applications in production-level automated vehicles including ADAS and DMS.
